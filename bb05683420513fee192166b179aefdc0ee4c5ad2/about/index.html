About the Study

Journey PRO is a study about chronic anemia and quality of life. In the study we will measure health and well-being using the Journey PRO app. We will track things like fatigue, memory, and fitness. You will help us learn about the range of experience for people with and without chronic anemia. You will help us find out how mobile devices can help measure what life is like with chronic anemia.

So long as you are 18 years old or older and have an iPhone model 5 or newer with iOS 8 or later, you can join Journey PRO. We specifically encourage people who have chronic anemia as a result of beta thalassemia, myelodysplastic syndrome (MDS), or myelofibrosis to join.

How the Study Works

Step 1

Download the App

In the study we will measure quality of life by looking at health and well-being. We will ask you to download the Journey PRO app

Step 2

Give consent to enroll

To get started, we will ask you to do an informed consent process. It takes about 20 minutes. Read the study consent form here. We will also ask you to do a one-time health survey. It takes about 20 minutes.

Step 3

Perform simple tasks

We will track things like fatigue, memory, and fitness through this app. We will ask you to complete a daily survey. You can choose the time of day you complete it. The survey takes about 5 minutes to complete.

Once a week, we will ask you to do a 40 minute challenge. The challenge has a survey with about 30 questions. The challenge also has activities: a few quick brain tests and a short walking activity. You can choose the day and time you do the weekly challenge.

Step 4

Track your health

We will also give some participants an Apple Watch to use as a fitness tracker. It will measure activity and heart rate, which are signs of health and well-being. We will look for patterns in the data.

Step 5

Scientists make discoveries

We hope that people will participate for at least 12 weeks in a row. This will help scientists look for patterns in the data. In this way, we hope to learn more about how chronic anemia impacts quality of life for people.

Who is Involved?

Sage Bionetworks, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit research organization, and its partners are conducting the Journey PRO research study. The Journey PRO research study is being funded by Celgene Corporation (www.celgene.com). Dr. Lara Mangravite is the Principal Investigator.

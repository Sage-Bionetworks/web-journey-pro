Thank you


Thank you for participating in the Journey PRO chronic anemia study. Your participation shed light on how chronic anemia affects a participant’s daily life. The study collected valuable data from your activities and logs that allowed us to measure the effects of chronic anemia. Thank you for your support and dedication!

We completed the study. We are not enrolling new participants. We are not going to collect any new study data through the Journey PRO mobile app, although you are free to use the app for as long as you like.

Thank you again for your participation. We could not have done this study without you!

Journey PRO Study Staff

Journey PRO is a research study and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
